How to install

Install Modpack

Click a modpack and follow the Modrinth page for downloads and installation instructions.

Resource Packs

Drop the resource pack into your `resourcepacks/` folder.

Datapacks

Place datapacks in your world's `datapacks/` folder and enable them.

Plugins

Follow server plugin docs; most are installed into the `plugins/` directory on your server.
